# Learning Log

Document your progress throughout the **12-week Aerospace Software Internship Preparation Roadmap**.

This directory serves as a personal engineering journal where you record what you learned, the projects you built, the challenges you solved, and how your skills evolved over time. By the end of the roadmap, it becomes a valuable record of your growth that can support internship applications, technical interviews, and portfolio discussions.

---

# Purpose

The learning log helps you:

- Track weekly learning progress
- Record concepts learned and mastered
- Document challenges and their solutions
- Keep a record of projects and assignments
- Track GitHub commits and milestones
- Monitor study hours and productivity
- Reflect on your progress each week
- Build interview-ready stories from real experiences
