# Reading List

## Core Aerospace Books

### Foundational
- **Introduction to Flight** - John D. Anderson
  - Chapters 1-5: Flight fundamentals, aerodynamics
  - Best for: Understanding lift, drag, thrust, weight
  - Time: 2 weeks

- **Orbital Mechanics for Engineering Students** - Howard Curtis
  - Chapters 1-3: Orbit basics
  - Best for: Satellite trajectories, orbital elements
  - Time: 3 weeks

- **Understanding Space: An Introduction to Astronautics** - Jerry Jon Sellers
  - Complete overview of space systems
  - Best for: Broad aerospace context
  - Time: 4 weeks

### Autonomous Systems
- **Probabilistic Robotics** - Thrun, Burgard, Fox
  - Chapters 1-7: State estimation, Kalman filters
  - Best for: Drone autonomy fundamentals
  - Time: 4 weeks

- **Planning Algorithms** - Steven LaValle
  - Chapters 1-6: Path planning, motion planning
  - Best for: Drone navigation and avoidance
  - Time: 6 weeks

## Programming & Software

- **Clean Code** - Robert Martin
  - Best for: Professional coding standards
  - Time: 2 weeks (skim)

- **Design Patterns** - Gang of Four
  - Best for: Large system architecture
  - Time: Optional, advanced
